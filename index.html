Coffee

Coffee is a beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans. It's also a plant "Coffea" and the name of the drink that is made from this plant.The drink is made from the seeds of the coffee plant, called coffee beans.
Coffee contains a chemical called caffeine, a mild drug that keeps people awake.
